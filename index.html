Company Overview

Our Journey

Depo Bangladesh is an online warehousing platform linking thousands of warehouses around the country with businesses with affordable and flexible warehousing solutions. Our journey has begun in 2020 with a clear mission to unify the warehousing industry under one platform using technology that offers hassle-free, cost-effective warehouses for our customers. At Depo Bangladesh, we are constantly developing our platform to enable superior customer experience and efficiency to your business. By working together closely with our partners and customers, we help businesses accelerate forward.


Our Warehouses

Depo Bangladesh introduced as the Next-Gen Warehousing Solution connects warehouse vendors with seekers, providing flexibility, affordability, accessibility in the traditional warehousing sector. The online booking system enables customers to select warehouses based on their distinct product requirements e.g. time duration for the warehouse, closest location to your business, perishable or non-perishable products etc. with convenient payment system through mobile financial services like Bkash, Nagad, Rocket and bank cheque or cash payment. Our innovative mobile app also enables constant monitoring and communications with our service team to ensure absolute assurance when it comes to your product safety.
